About Us

    PT Mulia Usaha Bersama, commonly referred to as PT MUB is located in Malang, a small picturesque mountain town that's famous for it's apples, cool climate and numerous cigarette factories. MUB is a manufacturing company that was founded in 2013 to help fulfull the demands of the cigarette industry in a rapidly growing domestic market. We produce filters which are used by many cigarette factories in Indonesia and across South East Asia. Our filters are produced to meet the highest of expectations, this is achieved through the hard work of our dedicated team, our stringent quality control systems, and commitment to use only the highest quality raw materials. At PT Mulia Usaha Bersama we currently have a monthly capacity of over 100,000,000 filter rods per month, which continues to grow as our reach expands internationally.

  Our company never stops striving to improve the quality of our products and services so we can better take care of the growing needs of our clients. To help achieve our mission to produce the highest quality filters in the market we continue to place great importance in the research and development conducted in our lab, we regularly update our production machinery and quality control equipment, while always listening to the feedback and comments of our customers.

  We are confident that our filters will add value to your cigarettes, ensuring that your customers continue to enjoy and smoke your brand.
